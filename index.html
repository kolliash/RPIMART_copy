Welcome to RPI Market Place!

Introducing RPI Market! RPI Market is a student-run web application designed exclusively for the RPI community, allowing you to easily buy, sell, rent, and give away items in a safe environment. With dedicated tabs for transactions like Buy/Sell, Rent/Loan, and Free Stuff, finding what you need is a breeze. Our tag system helps you categorize listings effortlessly. Plus, by registering with your RPI email, you ensure that all users are part of our trusted community. Join RPI Market today and discover a reliable way to connect with fellow students and faculty!
